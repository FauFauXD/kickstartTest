    MAD RUSH!

    THE DEBUT album from Madness, "One Step Beyond", was rushed into the shops in double-quick time even for Stiff Records. Mixing was completed on a Friday morning and the master disc was rushed to the CBS pressing factory in Aylesbury. There was a hitch when it was promptly dropped and shattered into a thousand splinters but a second master was cut and hurried back the same day. The sleeves and labels were finished the following Monday, 23,000 copies were pressed on Tuesday and the first were in the shops by Friday. But is it any good?
